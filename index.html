Tiger Shroff is known as a great dancer courtesy his flexible moves in “Heropanti” and various online videos, and the actor says he wishes to open a dance school to train talent across the country. The young actor is not a fan of various dance competition shows and thinks an institute would be a better platform for budding performers. “I wanted to open up a dance school for everybody including the underprivileged.
